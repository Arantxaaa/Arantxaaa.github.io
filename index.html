How I Work

My photographic process is very simple, i have a conversation with my consumer and try to get as much information about the product they wish for. After that i get to work and try to get the final product done in a timely matter and at the highest quality. My goal is satisfy the consumer at the best of my ability and if the consumer is not completely satisfied i will keep working until they are. I generally enjoy meeting with the consumer more than once to acquired feedback and also to become more familliar with them so we can both be very comfortable and get to know each other.
